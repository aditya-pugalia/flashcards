## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**How does AI card generation work?**  
Paste your notes into the Magic Generate screen and the app will
automatically turn them into question-and-answer flashcards.

**Is my data stored online?**  
Your decks are saved locally on your device. Only the text you
submit for AI generation is sent to a secure server to process
your request.

**How do I delete a deck?**  
Press and hold a deck on the home screen to reveal the delete option.
